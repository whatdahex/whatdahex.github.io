Day 1:

Breakfast:

  • Omelette of 3 egg whites and one whole egg. Mix in veggies like spinach, capsicum, onions, coriander, green chillis. Some salt and pepper for taste.

  • Wash it down with a teaspoon of apple cider vinegar in half a glass of water.

Lunch:

  • Rice cooked (150 gms) with 1 cup of daal (lentils) and sauteed broccoli. A lot of water during the day.

Dinner:

  • One big bowl of vegetable soup with broccoli, capsicum, lettuce, celery, onion, carrots. To spice it up, pepper, chillis, black salt and chilli flakes.

  • Chiken tikka (150 gms)
